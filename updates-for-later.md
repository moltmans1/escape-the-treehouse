These are the other paths of the puzzle sequence that we removed for simplicity.  Putting them here to add back later.


### Path B: The Statue & The Password
1.  **The Statue**: A wooden owl statue on a shelf points its eyes/beak towards a distant tree branch visible through the window.
2.  **The Binoculars**: The player uses binoculars to look out the window in the direction the statue is pointing, revealing a hidden code (e.g., a house number carved into the distant tree).
3.  **The Password**: This code is entered into the digital lock behind the dartboard to unlock the trapdoor.

### Path C: The Closet & Zipline Harness
1.  **Telescope Math**: The player finds a stars/constellation book. Aligning the telescope to specific coordinates using clues from the book reveals a hidden key (the Closet Key).
2.  **The Closet**: The Closet Key opens the locked closet. Inside is a sturdy wooden box containing the zipline harness, but it is bound tightly with thick cords.
3.  **Hanger Puzzle**: Looking up, the player sees a clue drawn on the closet ceiling. They rotate the clothes hangers in the closet to match the clue, which pops open a small compartment containing scissors.
4.  **Free the Harness**: The player uses the scissors to cut the cords and retrieve the Zipline Harness.

### Final Escape
1.  Entering the password from Path B into the digital lock opens the trapdoor, allowing the player to climb the ladder to the lookout platform.
2.  From the lookout, the player uses a telescope to locate the zipline.
3.  Equipping the Zipline Harness, they click the zipline to escape and win the game!
